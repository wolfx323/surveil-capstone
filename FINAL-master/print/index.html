PRINT COMPONENT

The printed zine portion of the project consisted of the bulk of the project. This was the vehicle to communicate information about data privacy in an interesting and visually appealing format. The length is a quick 36 pages of content, using experimental typography and photo editing.

This was printed at the Minneapolis College of Art & Design's service bureau using a Riso EZ 591-U printer using mint and black inks, "Shocking Green" Glo-Tone French Paper, and includes an inner "drop card" that is sticker paper adhered to silver mirror board.
